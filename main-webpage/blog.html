Tuna: A Day In The Life

Ever wonder what the tuna you're eating did before it ended up on your plat?

The answer is -- quite a lot, actually!

Tuna are very active fish in the sea. They are known to travel far distances deep below the oceans surface. Tuna primarily eat zooplankton and as a result can grow to be very large in size.

Unfortuantley, over-fishing is a large problem for the species as a whole, so tuna must constantly be wary of their surroundings so that they do not get cought. We at DevOps Sushi has a a large commitment to sustainable fishing practices because we wouuld like everyone to be able to enjoy sushi for many generations to come.

Now you know a little more about the food that you love to eat and that we love to prepare.
