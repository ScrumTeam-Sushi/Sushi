Our Beginnings; Why Sushi?

Our Sushi passions brought us together. As children, all three of the founders developed a passion for sushi whcih we each used to create with our families. Our long-held family traditions of sushi prompted us to enter our spiritual sushi journey to Japan where we studied the ancient arts of sushi for years and years. Now, that we've developed our skills, we have decieded to return to Washington, DC to share our fantastic sushi with YOU, our prized customer. Enjoy!
